Overview

One of the main characters in the Omniverse. Lionel's original name could be Jay, based on a potential typo in the written story.

History

Lionel on entry to the bunker wanted to reinvent himself with a new name, unfortunately Lionel Richie was all he could think of.

Lionel was living in the conclave working on keeping the neural microchips, that powered the maintenance AI, in working order. Until one day when he saw a random message on his terminal from his supposed neighbor. Ellie, his neighbor in 3C, tricked Lionel into breaking them out of the the conclave and into the irradiated wasteland of Earth.
