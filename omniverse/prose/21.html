“70% nitrogen, 20% oxygen, 10% casual racism and smoked meats.” Heath reported.

It showed a robot being hung by its own cables, with the slogan ‘The only place for Robos is silicon hell’ scrawled in engine oil underneath. The word silicon had been misspelt and scratched out six times. The word ‘for’ was also a number, but unfortunately it was the number three. Turns out they were stupid as well as racist.

“Seems like home. There were signs just like that demonising my people all over Ithia before Hallie stopped the witchhunters. Every head raised slightly as they passed, their dead green eyes following them, until they came to a screen with constantly rotating algorithms and calculations on it, also reruns of the Golden Girls in a corner.

“For some reason Dororthy speaks to me. She might be the only being in the Omniverse more evil than I.” The Technomancer pointed to one rotating diagram. It was a moebius strip representing the heat death of the universe, of all universes.in the centre, the twist was the crunch the crew she had seen with Lionel, Doots, and Mookie. The crunch that would kill them all bar the Doots and Techomancers of their respective realities.

“What is this revelation you’ve come to?” Ellie said, almost fearing the answer.

“Your friends want to stop this cycle that we find ourselves in, a cycle that has less than 50 Terran solar cycles left.” The Technomancer smiled again, and Ellie could tell he was genuine. “We don’t have to fight or struggle anymore, Ellie. You see, I want to do exactly the same.”

THE END
